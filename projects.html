About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Nicholas Knight, and I am passionate about both business management and technology. With a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration Management from St. Petersburg College, I have cultivated a unique skill set that merges business strategy with expertise in programming, web development, and data analysis. I recently updated this portfolio site, and although the projects shown here reflect earlier work, they will soon showcase my latest achievements.

Having grown up in an environment rich in IT and business management, I’ve honed my skills by creating various queries, websites, and e-commerce stores using technologies such as HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL. Additionally, I am actively involved in the JavaScript programming community, collaborating on projects that range from gaming applications to web development and data solutions. This portfolio is designed to offer a glimpse into my skill set and experience.

Key Skills & Expertise

Business Management & Data Analysis: Managed teams, leveraging leadership and communication skills to drive operational success. Utilized SQL, T-SQL, and SSMS to design, manage, and execute complex queries for business insights. Expertise in Tableau Dashboards for visualizing large datasets and extracting key business metrics.



Programming & Web Development: Proficient in Python, JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS for full-stack development. Entrepreneurship & E-Commerce: Founder and owner of Man-Kave.com, Knight Group, and WyGos, businesses focused on e-commerce and business development.
